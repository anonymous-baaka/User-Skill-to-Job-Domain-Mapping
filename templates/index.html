Mapping User Skills To Predict Job Domains.

Choosing a specific job domain to make a perfect career is a very challenging task for all IT Graduates these days due to the availability of innumerous job domains and new emerging technologies.

Our website helps you to go through different assesments to analyze the results and predict the best suitable job in the IT field by providing a free report on all the skills and data collected through these assesments.

About us

Jobmap is a website made with a clear motto of helping all the engineering graduates to choose a suitable job domain in the IT field with the help of various assesments and powerful prediction algorithm and provides a proper report specifying the results of the analysis.

The assesments involved include testing of both technical and soft skills to understand the strong and weak subjects of the user and accordingly these pointers are used to predict the results.

You can avail this free service byt just signing up by filling a few details followed by attempting the various assesments involved in the process.
